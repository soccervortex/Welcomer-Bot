Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 18-11-2024

1. Introduction

Welcomer Bot is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and how it is protected.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following data to provide our services:

  • Server IDs and channel IDs for configuration purposes.
  • Custom welcome messages and background settings set by server administrators.
  • Temporary data such as usernames and profile pictures for welcome messages.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to operate the Bot's features. No data is shared with third parties.

4. Data Retention

Configuration data is stored securely and retained as long as the Bot remains in your server. Upon removal, all associated data is deleted within 24 hours.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request data deletion by removing the Bot from your server.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Continued use of the Bot constitutes your acceptance of any changes.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at Support Mail.
